BreakZone

BreakZone is dedicated to safeguarding the rich culinary heritage of Haiti's diverse cities. Our dynamic web app, powered by Cloudinary, MongoDB, EJS, CSS, JavaScript, and utilizing SendGrid for seamless user verification, along with Passport for authentication, curates and preserves the authentic flavors of Haitian dishes. We combine technology and tradition to celebrate the unique gastronomic traditions found in each city, from the vibrant street food of Port-au-Prince to the mouthwatering delicacies of Cap-Haïtien. BreakZone is your gateway to exploring and cherishing the remarkable culinary treasures of Haiti, ensuring that the legacy of Haitian cuisine thrives for generations to come.
